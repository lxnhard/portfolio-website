About me

My name is Leonhard Link. I am a certified fullstack web developer from Berlin, Germany.

I am currently most comfortable with the frontend framework React as well as NodeJS, Express, classic Javascript, HTML and (S)CSS. Being enthusiastic about anything 3D, I am presently diving into ThreeJS and WebGL.

Before getting into web development, I have worked several years as an online marketing manager. During my career, I have contributed to new and existing web apps and websites in different roles, ranging from content creation over SEO to project management. Having worked closely with software engineers and designers, I made the choice to swap sides and become a developer myself.

I am familiar with a broad range of web technologies, including:

HTML5 · CSS · JavaScript · React · Redux · Angular · NodeJS · Express · MongoDB · AWS · TDD · BDD · Jest

I have over 4 years of experience in digital marketing and communication. Having worked on numerous websites and apps in different roles, I am well versed in technical SEO and know how to communicate efficiently with clients, designers and developers.

Besides being a certified fullstack developer, I have a master's degree in history. While utilizing digital technology for historical research (text mining and NLP), I have worked for Germany's predominant information platform for historical research.
