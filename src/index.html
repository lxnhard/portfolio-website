About me

My name is Leonhard Link. I am a certified fullstack web developer from Berlin, Germany.

After completing a comprehensive coding bootcamp, I primarily work with the frontend framework React as well as NodeJS, Express and MongoDB. Besides that, I am enthusiastic about cloud computing and AWS.

Before diving into web development, I have worked as an online marketing manager in the health and social sector. My expertise in that field involves project management, content management and SEO for websites and web apps. Having worked closely with software engineers and designers, I decided to swap sides.

Besides being a fullstack developer, I have a master's degree in history.
