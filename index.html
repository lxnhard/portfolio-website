About me

HTML5 · CSS · JavaScript · React · Redux · Angular · NodeJS · Express · MongoDB · AWS · TDD · BDD · Jest

I have over 4 years of experience in digital marketing and communication. Having worked on numerous websites and apps in different roles, I am well versed in technical SEO and know how to communicate efficiently with clients, designers and developers.
